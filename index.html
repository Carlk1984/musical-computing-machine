Understanding Backlinks

Backlinks, or inbound links, are essential for SEO. They are created when one website links to another, signaling the content's quality and relevance to search engines like Google.

How Backlinks Work

When search engine bots crawl a webpage, they follow the links on that page to discover new content. If a static webpage links to another site, the search engine can follow that link and recognize it as a backlink. The value of a backlink depends on the quality and relevance of the linking page, with high-quality backlinks from authoritative sites providing significant SEO benefits.
